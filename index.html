Top 6 Workout Plans to Start Your Journey

1. Full Body Strength

Beginner

Workout Split:

  • 3 days per week
  • Full body each session
  • 45-60 minutes per workout

Key Exercises:

  • Squats
  • Push-ups
  • Dumbbell Rows
  • Plank
  • Lunges

2. Cardio & Core

Beginner

Workout Split:

  • 4 days per week
  • Alternating cardio and core
  • 30-45 minutes per workout

Key Exercises:

  • Jump Rope
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Russian Twists
  • Bicycle Crunches
  • High Knees

3. Upper/Lower Split

Intermediate

Workout Split:

  • 4 days per week
  • Upper/Lower split
  • 60-75 minutes per workout

Key Exercises:

  • Bench Press
  • Deadlifts
  • Pull-ups
  • Leg Press
  • Shoulder Press

4. Push/Pull/Legs

Intermediate

Workout Split:

  • 6 days per week
  • Push/Pull/Legs rotation
  • 60-90 minutes per workout

Key Exercises:

  • Barbell Squats
  • Barbell Rows
  • Overhead Press
  • Lat Pulldowns
  • Leg Curls

5. High-Intensity Interval Training

Advanced

Workout Split:

  • 5 days per week
  • HIIT focused
  • 30-45 minutes per workout

Key Exercises:

  • Burpees
  • Box Jumps
  • Kettlebell Swings
  • Battle Ropes
  • Sprint Intervals

6. Core & Flexibility

Beginner

Workout Split:

  • 3-4 days per week
  • Core and flexibility focus
  • 30-45 minutes per workout

Key Exercises:

  • Plank variations
  • Yoga poses
  • Russian twists
  • Leg raises
  • Dynamic stretching

Food Meal Plans

Weight Loss Plan

Daily Calorie Target: 1500-1800 kcal

Breakfast (400 kcal)
  • Oatmeal with berries and nuts
  • Greek yogurt with honey
  • Green tea or black coffee
Lunch (500 kcal)
  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Quinoa or brown rice
  • Steamed vegetables
  • Mixed salad with olive oil
Dinner (400 kcal)
  • Baked salmon
  • Sweet potato
  • Roasted vegetables
Snacks (200 kcal)
  • Fruits (apple, banana, berries)
  • Nuts (almonds, walnuts)
  • Vegetable sticks with hummus

Muscle Gain Plan

Daily Calorie Target: 2500-3000 kcal

Breakfast (600 kcal)
  • Scrambled eggs with whole wheat toast
  • Protein smoothie with banana and peanut butter
  • Greek yogurt with granola
